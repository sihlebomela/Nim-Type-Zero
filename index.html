About

This is a web game based on the anime series 'Kakegurui', from episode 3 "The Untouchable Girl".

How the game works

There are 4 types cards 0, 1, 2 and 3



Each player gets 4 cards at hand.



In each turn, each player will play a single card from their hand.



Each turn adds the number of the played card to the total.
If the card played by the player brings the total to more than 9, the player loses.
